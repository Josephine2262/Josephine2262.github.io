As a master's student, I know how challenging graduate school can be and just wanted to create a space where fellow students could find useful study tips and support

A few ideas to relax


Meditation Self-care 101

Meditation is great for a few reasons:

  • It can be practiced whenever and wherever you want
  • It is free -unless you want to buy equipment
  • It will force you take time off work and pay more attnetion to your feelings

Sleep The key to happiness

You will probably be tempted to work late or catch-up with lectures, but do remember that sleep is a key part of your success in grad schoo: don't neglect it!

Eating Habits Listen to your mom :)

True enough, we rarely feel like eating broccoli during exam season, but keeping healthy eating habits will allow you to feel full of energy
